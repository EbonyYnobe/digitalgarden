### CO<sub>2</sub>
$n_{0} = 8 \times 3 = 24$
$n_{v} = 4 \times 1 + 6 \times 2 = 16$
$n_{s} = 24-16 = 8 \quad \Rightarrow \quad \text{4 bonds}$
$n_{l} = 16-8 = 8 \quad \Rightarrow \quad \text{4 lone pairs}$
